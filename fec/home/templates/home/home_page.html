Welcome to beta.fec.gov

This site is in beta, which means we’re actively working on it and adding new features.  The official site for the Federal Election Commission is www.FEC.gov

About this site

We're working hard to make this a useful addition to the resources available at FEC.gov. Do you have feedback or feature requests? Email to let us know what works and what needs improvement.

Current features

  • Search for candidates and committees
  • Browse and filter candidates and committees
  • View redesigned candidate and committee pages
  • View all of a candidate’s authorized and joint fundraising committees
  • View latest report
  • A public API

Coming soon

  • Customizable calendar
  • Filing instructions for more committees
  • HTML campaign guides

Need help understanding terms?

If you encounter a technical word or a phrase you don’t know, this site’s glossary may be able to help. Click on words and phrases with the book icon next to them to pull up a definition. Or click the glossary icon at the top of any page to open the full, searchable glossary. For example, click on this term to see its definition: principal campaign committee.
