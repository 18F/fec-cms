Registration Options

By law, certain people and groups must register with the FEC and file regular reports about their campaign financial activity. Registrants range from individuals to candidates to political action committees (PACs). Every group has different registration and reporting rules. This is why it’s important to understand each and register correctly.

Not sure what to choose? Here’s a quick review:

Candidates and candidate committees

Candidates are people running for elected federal office. Federal office is the House of Representatives, the Senate, or the office of the president.

Candidates must designate at least one campaign committee. These authorized groups are called candidate committees.
